Cancellation and Refund Policy

Last Updated: 16th March 2025

At VSRP Innovations, we strive to provide high-quality services to our clients. This Cancellation and Refund Policy outlines the conditions under which cancellations and refunds may be requested.

1. Cancellation Policy

  • Clients may request a cancellation of services by providing written notice via email.
  • Cancellation requests must be submitted at least 7 days before the next billing cycle to avoid charges.
  • Once a service has been initiated, it cannot be canceled, and no refund will be provided.

2. Refund Policy

  • Refunds will only be issued in cases where VSRP Innovations fails to deliver the agreed-upon services.
  • Clients must submit a refund request within 7 days of service completion, providing clear reasons for the request.
  • Refunds will be processed within 10-15 business days after approval.
  • Partial refunds may be considered based on the extent of service utilization.

3. Non-Refundable Services

  • Custom data scraping solutions and consulting services are non-refundable.
  • Any third-party costs incurred on behalf of the client are non-refundable.
  • Refunds are not applicable in cases of policy violations or misuse of services.

4. Dispute Resolution

  • In case of disputes, clients are encouraged to contact us to resolve the issue amicably.
  • All refund disputes shall be governed by the laws of India and handled in the courts of Delhi, India.
